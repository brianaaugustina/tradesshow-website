Meet the Host

Briana Ottoboni

Briana Ottoboni is the host and creator of The Trades Show. After being awe-inspired by the work of a local cobbler who transformed a pair of old loafers, she apprenticed with a shoe cobbler and developed a fascination for the artisan trades – and a determination to revive them to their original glory, just like the cobbler did to her shoes.

The Trades Show was born as a storytelling medium to highlight artisan pathways in a return to what it means to be human.

She is also the founder of The Corral, a national jobs board connecting artisans with work, building an artisan ecosystem purpose-built for a cultural moment that won't wait.
